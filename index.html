For the past 4 years I've worked in the mental health field, trying my best to help people through the hard times that life throws at us all. It's rewarding at its best, and often challenging and draining at it's worst. To relax I listen to electronic music and browse the internet. At some point, I had the idea to combine those last two things. After a few months of googling and poking a keyboard, and I made a website.
